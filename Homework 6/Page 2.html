The Tortoise and the Hare

"The Tortoise and the Hare" is a classic fable that teaches the valuable lesson of 'slow and steady wins the race.' In the story, the boastful and overconfident Hare challenges the slow-moving Tortoise to a race. The Hare, believing he is much faster, takes a nap during the race, while the Tortoise continues at a slow but steady pace. When the Hare wakes up and realizes his mistake, he rushes to the finish line, but it's too late. The Tortoise has already won, proving that consistency and determination are more important than speed.
